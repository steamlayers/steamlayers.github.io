  • Introduction

    You will learn the basics of MongoDB as well as the Aggregation Framework itself.

  • Aggregation Stages

    The course will guide you through all stages in the Aggregation Pipeline.

    From basic ones ($match, $out, $sample, etc.) to more complex ($lookup, $redact, $facet, etc.).

  • Practical Applications

    The most important part.

    You will apply your knowledge to real-world problems, such as: pagination, data normalization, data denormalization, cross-collection queries, and more.

  • Toolchain

    By the end of this course, you will have a solid toolchain for testing, debugging, and profiling your aggregation queries.

    Everything will be as easy as running one command in a terminal.
